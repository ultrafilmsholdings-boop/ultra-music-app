About Ultra Music Group™

Ultra Music Group is an independent creative music label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa and built from the ground up to develop original artists, release new music, and create a unified world of sound, visuals, character, and identity through visual storytelling.

The label is designed as a full creative universe where every artist begins as an idea — a personality, a perspective, a sound, and a world. From concept to creation, Juan Pablo develops each project from the ground up: writing the music, shaping the artistic identity, designing the visual atmosphere, and building a catalog of songs that express emotion, movement, imagination, and cinematic energy.

Ultra Music Group spans multiple genres and creative lanes, including pop, cinematic pop, dance pop, crossover pop, Latin pop, reggaeton, Latin urban, Latin dance, electronic music, euphoric music, R&B, soul, blues, jazz, country, pop-country, regional Mexican, norteño, banda, banda norteña, ranchera, corridos, and genre-blending contemporary music built for both mainstream and character-driven storytelling.

What defines Ultra Music Group is not just the variety of its sound, but the intention behind the work. Every release is part of a larger creative vision — from the music itself to the visuals, artist development, branding, cinematic presentation, and digital experience. The goal is not simply to release songs, but to build an immersive label ecosystem where music and imagination come together in a way that feels alive.

Transparency, creativity, and artistic experimentation are core values of Ultra Music Group. Some productions use AI-assisted vocal technology and digital performance tools, and that process is openly shared with the audience. Technology is treated as a creative instrument — just like a synthesizer, microphone, camera, or studio — while the emotional core, writing, and vision remain at the center of every project.

Ultra Music Group is built for listeners who want more than a single track. It is a label universe designed around sound, atmosphere, character, movement, and emotional impact.

Music should inspire people to feel alive. Founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa.

ULTRA MUSIC GROUP™ — Mission Statement

Ultra Music Group is an independent creative music label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa, a songwriter, creator, and developer dedicated to building a new generation of musical storytelling through virtual artists and digital performers.

At Ultra Music Group, every artist begins as an idea — a character, a personality, and a world.

From concept to creation, Juan Pablo develops each project from the ground up: writing the music, shaping the identity of the artist, designing the visual universe, and building a catalog of songs that express emotion, movement, and imagination.

Transparency, creativity, and artistic experimentation are core values of Ultra Music Group.

Some Ultra Music Group productions use AI-assisted vocal technology and digital performance tools, and we openly share that process with our audience. Technology is simply a creative instrument — just like a synthesizer, microphone, or studio.

What defines Ultra Music Group is not the technology behind the music, but the heart behind the creation.

Every song, every artist, and every project released by Ultra Music Group is part of a larger vision — a creative universe where characters, music, and imagination come together to form something greater than any single song.

At the center of that vision is one guiding belief:

Music should inspire people to feel alive.

Founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa
