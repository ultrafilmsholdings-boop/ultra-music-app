About Ultra Music Group

Ultra Music Group is an independent creative music label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa, built from the ground up to develop original artists, release music, and create a unified world of sound, visuals, and identity.

The label brings together a growing roster across pop, Latin, and electronic music, focused on strong songwriting, character-driven artists, and cinematic presentation.

Every release is part of a larger creative vision — combining music, storytelling, visuals, and brand development into one evolving universe.

Mission Statement

Ultra Music Group is dedicated to building a new generation of musical storytelling through virtual artists and digital performers.

Every artist begins as an idea — a character, a personality, and a world.

From concept to creation, each project is developed from the ground up: writing the music, shaping the identity, designing the visual universe, and building a catalog that expresses emotion, movement, and imagination.

Some productions use AI-assisted vocal technology and digital tools, openly shared as part of the creative process. Technology is treated as an instrument — just like a microphone or studio.

What defines Ultra Music Group is not the technology behind the music, but the heart behind the creation.

Every release is part of a larger vision — where music, characters, and imagination come together to form something greater than any single song.

Founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa.
