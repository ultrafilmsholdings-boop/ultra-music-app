About Ultra Music Group

Ultra Music Group is an independent creative music label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa and built from the ground up to develop original artists, release new music, and create a unified world of sound, visuals, and identity.

The label brings together a growing roster across pop, Latin, electronic, and crossover music, with a focus on strong songwriting, memorable characters, cinematic presentation, and long-term world-building.

Every release is part of a larger creative vision — from the music itself to the visuals, artist development, branding, and digital experience. Ultra Music Group is designed as a next-generation independent label built on creativity, consistency, innovation, and emotional storytelling.

Independent label concept, music development, and creative direction by Juan Pablo Ochoa.

Mission Statement

Ultra Music Group is an independent creative music label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa, a songwriter, creator, and developer dedicated to building a new generation of musical storytelling through virtual artists and digital performers.

  • At Ultra Music Group, every artist begins as an idea — a character, a personality, and a world.
  • From concept to creation, Juan Pablo develops each project from the ground up: writing the music, shaping the identity of the artist, designing the visual universe, and building a catalog of songs that express emotion, movement, and imagination.
  • Transparency, creativity, and artistic experimentation are central to the label’s foundation.
  • Some Ultra Music Group productions use AI-assisted vocal technology and digital performance tools, and that process is shared openly with the audience. Technology is treated as a creative instrument — just like a synthesizer, microphone, or studio.

What defines Ultra Music Group is not the technology behind the music, but the heart behind the creation.

Every song, every artist, and every project released by Ultra Music Group is part of a larger vision — a creative universe where characters, music, and imagination come together to form something greater than any single song.

Founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa.
