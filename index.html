About Ultra Music Group

Ultra Music Group is an independent record label founded by Juan Pablo Ochoa, built to create original music, develop artists, and build a unified creative universe.

Mission Statement

Every artist begins as a concept — a character, a world, a vision. Music, visuals, and identity are all created together as one experience.
