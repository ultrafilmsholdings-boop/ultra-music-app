Founder Artist

Juan Pablo

Pop artist, songwriter, producer, and label founder building a full music experience through vision, storytelling, and consistency.

Artist Bio

Juan Pablo is a pop artist, songwriter, producer, and creative founder whose work is driven by scale, identity, and long-term vision. Rather than approaching music as a series of disconnected releases, he builds songs, artists, branding, and presentation as part of one larger world. That perspective is central to both his own music and the overall direction of Ultra Music Group, where he leads with the mindset of creating a polished audience experience instead of simply uploading songs and hoping they find an audience on their own.

His sound is rooted in polished pop, strong melodies, and cinematic framing. The records are intended to feel clean, direct, emotional, and memorable, with enough structure to connect to a broad audience while still carrying a personal point of view. As a songwriter and producer, he focuses not only on how a track sounds in the moment, but on how it supports an entire identity around the artist, the campaign, and the bigger future of the label.

Beyond his own catalog, Juan Pablo is building a platform where multiple artists can live under a unified, elevated brand. That means shaping visuals, presentation, messaging, rollout strategy, and the premium feel of the fan experience itself. His role is not limited to making songs. He is positioning Ultra Music Group as a destination where fans can discover artists, follow releases, and connect with a stronger, more intentional version of independent music culture.

With a focus on consistency, quality control, and expansion, Juan Pablo continues to build both as an artist and as a founder. The goal is clear: create music that reaches people, create artists that feel distinctive, and create a label identity that can grow into something much larger over time without losing the clarity of its original vision.

Pop Artist

Lily Heart

A warm, polished pop presence centered on emotion, connection, and songs that stay close to the listener.

Artist Bio

Lily Heart is a pop artist whose music is built around emotion, softness, and connection. Her sound is designed to feel intimate without losing polish, giving listeners songs that are melodic, personal, and easy to return to. She brings a presence that feels sincere and inviting, and that quality allows her music to create a closer relationship with the audience instead of simply passing through as background.

Her records focus on love, vulnerability, reflection, and the emotional details that define everyday experiences. Rather than overcomplicating the message, Lily Heart’s style centers on clarity and feeling. The result is music that feels open, expressive, and emotionally direct while still carrying the clean, elevated presentation expected from a premium pop release.

As part of Ultra Music Group, Lily Heart represents the label’s commitment to artist distinction and tonal variety. She brings a softer emotional lane to the roster while maintaining the same overall standard of branding, quality, and audience connection. Her releases are meant to feel cohesive, visually strong, and aligned with a broader vision that gives her room to grow over time.

Lily Heart continues to shape a catalog that values meaning as much as melody. Her work speaks to listeners who want music that feels personal, beautiful, and genuine, while also presenting a refined artistic identity that can expand with each release.

Neon Euphoria

Bright energy, bold atmosphere, and high-impact production built to feel colorful and alive.

Artist Bio

Neon Euphoria is built around energy, color, atmosphere, and the kind of sound that immediately changes the temperature of a room. The project lives in a lane where polished production, vibrant pacing, and bold emotional tone come together to create music that feels cinematic, euphoric, and modern without losing its replay value.

The identity behind Neon Euphoria is not meant to feel flat or anonymous. It is meant to feel immersive. Every release is an invitation into a heightened mood, whether through synth texture, movement, intensity, or the overall visual and sonic character of the project. This makes the artist especially strong for branding, digital presentation, and larger world-building across the label.

Within Ultra Music Group, Neon Euphoria expands the roster into a more colorful and high-energy space. The project brings variety, contrast, and a more electrified emotional tone while still fitting under the same polished presentation that defines the label. That balance is important because it lets the catalog feel diverse without losing its overall identity.

Neon Euphoria continues to develop as a strong concept-driven act for listeners who want sound that feels vibrant, elevated, and immediate. The goal is not to be generic background music. The goal is to create a feeling, hold it, and leave a strong visual and sonic impression every time.

Regional Mexican

Ángel Valentín

A voice shaped by strength, tradition, and emotional storytelling with bold regional character.

Artist Bio

Ángel Valentín is a regional Mexican artist whose music carries tradition, passion, and emotional storytelling with clarity and force. His sound is grounded in cultural strength while remaining accessible to broader audiences through songs that speak to resilience, love, pride, and personal identity. That balance gives his music both authenticity and reach.

What defines Ángel Valentín is not only the style of the music, but the conviction behind it. The voice, the message, and the emotional framing all work together to create an artist identity that feels strong and memorable. His releases are meant to connect with listeners who value both traditional influence and polished presentation, allowing the music to feel rooted without feeling stuck in the past.

As part of Ultra Music Group, Ángel Valentín expands the label into an important lane of sound and audience connection. He represents range within the roster while still fitting the larger standard of brand quality, artist development, and release strategy. His place in the label helps communicate that Ultra Music Group is not limited to one tone, one genre, or one type of listener.

Ángel Valentín continues building a catalog meant to last, with songs that carry emotional weight, personal character, and a strong sense of identity. The aim is to create records that feel honest, powerful, and lasting while growing an artist profile that stands out within the genre.
